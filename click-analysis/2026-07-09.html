Read This Before Trusting the Numbers

Why email is under-credited in GA

GA's "direct" bucket is a junk drawer: typed URLs, bookmarks, privacy browsers, ad tags that fell off, and email clicks whose referrer got stripped in transit. We can't say what slice of those 546 direct sessions is email, and we won't guess. But the fingerprint is clear: the lead pulled 153 web users in GA against roughly 149 unique email clickers in Beehiiv, while GA tagged only ~37 sessions as newsletter. Those readers came from the email; GA just lost the label. Bottom line: trust Beehiiv for email engagement, trust GA for non-email demand (Nextdoor, search, ads), and remember external links (venues, bookstore site) never touch this property at all.
